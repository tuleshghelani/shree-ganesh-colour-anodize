Our Advanced Chrome Plating Process

1. Surface Preparation

Meticulous cleaning and preparation of metal surfaces to remove contaminants and ensure optimal chrome adhesion and finish quality.

2. Base Layer Application

For decorative applications, precision application of copper and nickel underlayers to enhance corrosion resistance and surface quality.

3. Chrome Electroplating

Controlled immersion in chromium solution with precise electrical current application to deposit a uniform chrome layer of the specified thickness.

4. Post-Plating Treatment

Specialized rinsing and neutralizing processes to remove residual chemicals and stabilize the chrome surface for optimal performance.

5. Quality Assurance

Comprehensive testing and inspection to verify plating thickness, adhesion, appearance, and performance characteristics meet all specifications.
